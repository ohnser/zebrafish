About

My work focuses on designing experimental systems that combine precise stimulus control, high-speed imaging, and automated behavioral analysis. These tools support studies of auditory and vestibular function in larval zebrafish and related model systems.

Featured Work

Selected areas of development across hardware, software, imaging, and behavioral analysis.

Zebrafish VOR System

Instrumentation for vestibular stimulation and quantitative eye movement analysis.

  • 3D eye modeling and reconstruction
  • Rotational stimulus control
  • Automated response quantification

Startle & GPIAS Platform

Behavioral platform for acoustic startle and gap prepulse inhibition experiments.

  • Audio stimulus generation
  • LED-based timing synchronization
  • C-bend and locomotion analysis

OKR Imaging Setup

Visual stimulation and imaging system for optokinetic response measurement.

  • Visual stimulus presentation
  • Eye tracking workflow
  • Analysis pipeline development
